About Senmi

Who We Are

Senmi is a technology-driven logistics and delivery platform focused on helping individuals and businesses move packages quickly, safely, and efficiently.

We understand the importance of speed, trust, and communication in modern delivery services. Our platform is designed to simplify logistics by connecting customers with verified riders in real time.

From personal deliveries to business logistics, Senmi provides reliable solutions powered by technology, transparency, and customer satisfaction.
