Senmi Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Welcome to Senmi, a platform operated by Real Time Delivery App Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose your information when you use the Senmi mobile application, website, rider services, customer delivery services, and related technologies.

By using Senmi, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect different categories of information including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery addresses
  • Pickup information
  • Location and GPS data
  • Payment information
  • Device information
  • FCM notification tokens
  • Rider verification documents
  • Profile photos and uploaded images
  • Transaction and delivery history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide real-time delivery services
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Match customers with riders
  • Process payments and withdrawals
  • Verify rider identities
  • Track deliveries and rider locations
  • Improve platform security
  • Send important notifications
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve application performance

3. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate pickup and delivery details
  • Ensure phone numbers are reachable during delivery
  • Avoid fraudulent payment activities
  • Respect riders and platform staff
  • Provide correct package descriptions
  • Not use Senmi for illegal deliveries
  • Ensure packages comply with local laws

4. Rider Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate rider verification information
  • Operate responsibly and professionally
  • Maintain respectful communication with customers
  • Protect customer packages during delivery
  • Follow traffic and transportation laws
  • Avoid fraudulent delivery claims
  • Keep rider profile information updated
  • Maintain confidentiality of customer information

5. Payments

Senmi may process payments through third-party payment providers. By using our services, you agree that:

  • Payment information may be processed securely by payment gateways
  • Withdrawal requests may require account verification
  • Fraudulent payment activity may lead to suspension
  • Delivery charges may change over time
  • Refund requests are subject to review

6. Notifications

Senmi uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and other technologies to send notifications including:

  • Delivery updates
  • Order status notifications
  • Account approval notifications
  • Security alerts
  • Promotional announcements
  • Administrative broadcasts

Users may disable notifications in device settings, although some critical service notifications may still be required.

7. Account Suspension

Senmi reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts involved in:

  • Fraudulent activity
  • Fake rider verification
  • Abusive behavior
  • Illegal package delivery
  • Repeated violation of platform policies

  • Misuse of the application
