1. Customer Support

Customers can contact Senmi support for assistance with:

  • Package tracking issues
  • Delayed deliveries
  • Payment verification
  • Failed transactions
  • Refund requests
  • Incorrect delivery information
  • Missing packages
  • Delivery disputes
  • Account login issues
  • Notification problems

Our support team aims to respond to urgent delivery issues as quickly as possible to ensure smooth package operations.

2. Rider Support

Riders using the Senmi platform may contact support for:

  • Rider profile approval
  • Verification review updates
  • Wallet and earnings issues
  • Withdrawal delays
  • Delivery assignment concerns
  • Account restrictions
  • Technical problems with the rider app
  • GPS and live tracking issues
  • Notification delivery issues
  • Safety and emergency concerns

Approved riders are encouraged to keep profile information accurate and maintain professional conduct during all deliveries.

3. Payment & Wallet Support

Senmi works with trusted payment providers to ensure secure transactions. If you experience payment-related issues, support is available for:

  • Failed Paystack payments
  • Duplicate charges
  • Payment verification problems
  • Wallet balance discrepancies
  • Withdrawal processing delays
  • Bank transfer issues
  • Fraud prevention investigations
Important: Senmi will never ask for your password, OTP, ATM PIN, or sensitive banking credentials.

4. Technical Support

If you encounter technical problems while using Senmi, our support engineers can help resolve:

  • Login or authentication errors
  • App crashes or freezing
  • Notification delivery issues
  • Tracking problems
  • Location permission errors
  • Upload and image submission failures
  • Slow app performance
  • Network synchronization issues

To help us investigate technical issues faster, please provide screenshots and a clear explanation of the problem when contacting support.

5. Safety & Security

Senmi prioritizes the safety of customers and riders. You should immediately contact support if you experience:

  • Suspicious account activity
  • Fraud attempts
  • Unauthorized transactions
  • Harassment or abusive behavior
  • Fake rider impersonation
  • Unsafe delivery conditions
  • Stolen or missing packages

Reports involving fraud or abuse may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform.

6. Response Time

Senmi aims to respond to support inquiries as quickly as possible. Estimated response times may vary depending on issue type:

  • Urgent delivery issues — within hours
  • Payment verification — same day
  • Rider approval reviews — 1 to 3 business days
  • Technical investigations — up to several business days
  • General inquiries — within 24 hours
